C5 Self Confidence

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Mindfulness practiced allows higher aware of emotions that one might be feeling at the time. This higher awareness of feelings leads to more accurate self assessment of one's strengths and weaknesses. A better understanding of self leads to higher confidence in one's abilities.
