C2 Emotional Intelligence

What learnings did you take from the chapter?

First off its great that EDA is 'getting us' to read this book! Emotional Intelligence consists of five components:

  1. Self Awareness
  2. Self Regulation
  3. Motivation
  4. Empathy
  5. Social Skills
High emotional intelligence is associated with stellar work performance, outstanding leadership and ability to create conditions for happiness. On the personal side, higher emotional intelligence can lead to persistent 'side-effects' like happiness, resilience, optimism and kindness.

Sections on training attention, bringing mindfulness to the body, neuroplasticity and neuroimaging therapy contain new-to-me, 'mind blowing' information. All the scientific helps the logical part of me buy into mindfulness techniques.

How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

Mindfulness clarifies my mind. As was mentioned with the snowglobe analogy, once the agitation stops it returns to its default state of calmness and tranquil.
