What new things have you learnt about meditation?

Meditation is nothing but practice. In other traditions meditation has synonyms such as 'Bhavana'/Cultivation & 'becoming aware'. Concentration is using meta-attention to focus attention. With concentration the mind can be allowed to settle leading to its default state of clarity, happiness and joy. Happy is thus something one allows not achieves. In meditation and exercise, the first week is easy, but long lasting results and fulfillment involves persisting past the first week.
Whilst one might want to follow the traditional standard meditation posture, in reality the best posture is whatever allows alertness and relaxation to occur at the same time.
